Our Services

Renting a property and paying a deposit in advance online could not end well. Most people decide to stay in a hotel or Airbnb for several weeks while finding themselves a place to rent, which could be costly in London.
We would visit the property on your behalf, you will not need to pay for accommodation and you could do it from the comfort of your own home. We will visit the advertised property and we will send you a report back.

Our report will contain pictures and a video of the surroundings and the entrance, which are not often posted online. We will let you know if the property looks like the advert. We will take pictures and photos on the day of the visit. Also, we will check things for you in advance: are the bathrooms clean? does the property look tidy? is it noisy? is it well insulated?

We are charging for visiting the property, so our main goal is to help you rent a place you will really like and enjoy while you stay in London.
