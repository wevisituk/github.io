About Us

We are locals that have been renting in London for over a decade in many different places, rooms shared accommodations, studios and entire properties from 1 to 4-bedroom houses. We know how difficult it is to find the right place.


The process could take weeks and the best places do not last long before they are rented out. If you are overseas and planning to move into London for work or to study, it is too risky to pay in advance for a room or a property without seeing yourself or without meeting with the agency or the landlord.


A safe option is to book a hotel or Airbnb for some time while visiting the different places, but that is expensive. We want to ease that stress and save you money: WeVisit for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

We have put together some commonly asked questions to give you more information about WeVisit and what we offer.

Central London and London Tube Zones 1-2.

We recommend SpareRoom for rooms in a shared property.
For entire properties like flats or houses, we recommend Rightmove, Zoopla and OpenRent.

Renting a property and paying a deposit in advance online could not end well. Most people decide to stay in a hotel or Airbnb for several weeks while finding themselves a place to rent, which could be costly in London.
We would visit the property on your behalf, you will not need to pay for accommodation and you could do it from your own home and country. We will visit the advertised property and we will send you a report back.

Our report will contain pictures and a video of the surroundings and the entrance, which are not often posted online. We will let you know if the property is as advertised and take pictures and photos on the day of the visit. We will also look for things for you in advance like if the bathrooms are clean, the property looks tidy or it is noisy and it is well insulated.

We are charging for visiting the property, so our main goal is to help you rent a place you will really like and enjoy while you stay in London.

We will not able to guarantee it 100%, but most of the scams are successful because the properties do not exist, or the landlord or letting agency is made up, etc. With locals like us, visiting the property and inspecting it, as well as meeting with the landlord or letting agency, the probabilities are much lower.
